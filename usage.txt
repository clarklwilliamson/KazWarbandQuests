KazWarbandQuests - Makes Dugi skip warband-completed quests

Hooks C_QuestLog.IsQuestFlaggedCompleted so Dugi treats warband-completed
quests as done on all characters. Excludes profession KP quests (those are
per-character and every crafter needs to do them).

Commands:
  /kwbq               Toggle on/off
  /kwbq on            Enable
  /kwbq off           Disable

  /kaz warband        Same as /kwbq
